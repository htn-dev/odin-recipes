Air Fryer Halloumi Cheese

Description

This is a breeze to make in the air fryer, and we like to serve it with sliced cherry tomatoes, freshly-chopped herbs or occasionally a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Ingredient

  • 8 ounces halloumi cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 360 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Slice halloumi into 6 equal slices, cut each slice in half, and dry with a paper towel. Brush all sides with olive oil.
  3. Lightly spray the air fryer basket with cooking spray. Place the halloumi slices in the basket, making sure they're not touching. You may have to cook in two batches,
  4. Cook for 7 to 9 minutes until golden brown, but make sure not to overcook or they turn rubbery. Serve immediately.
